# Notesnook

Notesnook is an open-source note-taking app focused on privacy: it encrypts
your notes before they leave your device (end-to-end encryption),
syncs them across web, desktop, and mobile apps, and includes features
like markdown, tasks, reminders, and file attachments, making it a secure
alternative to apps such as Evernote, OneNote, or Notion for people
who want their notes to stay private.
